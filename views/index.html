"International Mobile Equipment Identity" or IMEI is a distinctive 15-digit number that serves to identify a mobile device on a network. Similar to a social security number, it can be considered as the phone's unique identifier. It is allocated to each GSM phone, while MEID numbers are assigned to CDMA devices.

motivation

I am highly motivated to undertake a project that will help individuals who have lost their mobile phones due to theft or carelessness. This motivation stems from my personal experience when my friend lost his phone while traveling in a bus in Benglore city, presumably stolen by a pickpocket. I have realized the importance of a platform that can assist people in identifying whether the phone they have found or intend to buy is lost or stolen. Hence, I plan to develop a platform that will allow individuals to check whether a phone has been reported lost or stolen. Moreover, individuals can report their lost phones on our platform and keep track of how many times the same IMEI has been queried. I am committed to helping people safeguard their mobile phones and personal information from theft and misuse.

Before buying any second hand phone do check if it was stolen or misplaced by someone.
